MeKong Enterprise Fund

The Mekong Enterprise Fund was launched in 2002 at $18 million in committed capital and was fully invested in 10 companies by the end of 2005. The Fund invested in fast-growing unlisted companies in the Vietnamese private sector.

To learn more about the Fund's existing investments, please visit our Investments page.

Status of the Fund – The Fund's life is expected to be 10 years from the date of its launch on 22 April 2002. The Fund has fully divested from 4 companies in its portfolio and is focused on realizing the remaining holdings before the end of the Fund’s life based on detailed action plans.

Investment Universe and Selection Criteria

The Fund invested in the following investment universe and according to the following selection criteria:

  • The Fund was very selective about the investments it made, preferring to make fewer investments in order to only invest in excellent companies with a high likelihood of being successful investments. As a result none of the Fund's 10 investments have failed and all have grown since the Fund's initial investment;
  • The Fund invested only in private sector companies founded by local entrepreneurs instead of foreign-invested companies or formerly state-owned companies;
  • The Fund had a focused investment strategy targeting mostly manufacturing related businesses both for export and domestic sale;
  • The Fund targeted smaller sized companies, acquiring significant minority ownership;
  • The Fund aimed to invest in companies that have strong management teams and a commitment to continuously strengthening their management teams;
  • The Fund provides significant value-creation input to the companies in which it invested, in close partnership with those companies’ management teams.
