ImageCrop Default:

$('img').smImgCropper();

ImageCrop selects an area at the beginning:

$('img').eq(1).smImgCropper('cropImage',[50,50,200,200]);

ImageCrop randoms cropping area :

$('img').eq(2).smImgCropper('cropRandom');
