about us

       

Anova Corporation is proud to be one of the leading manufacturers in veterinary and aquaculture pharmaceutical technology. All the manufacturing processes strictly follow the quality standards of WHO-GMP management system. All the stages of research, manufacturing and packaging follow the most advanced and safest requirements in the world and are strictly controlled by the Quality Control Department.

Anova Corporation has 7 lines of medicine production, which are professional and most advanced in the world. Each line in exclusively designed for the production of each kind of products to prevent the cross-contamination.
